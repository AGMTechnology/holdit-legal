# HoldIt Support

Need help with HoldIt? We're here for you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does face detection work?**
HoldIt uses your front camera to detect facial expressions like smiles and laughs during gameplay. All processing happens on your device — no photos or videos are ever saved or sent anywhere.

**Why does the app need camera access?**
The camera is used only during gameplay to detect if you laugh or smile. You can deny camera access, but face detection won't work without it.

**How do I report inappropriate content?**
Tap the flag icon in the top-right corner of any content during gameplay to report it. We review all reports and remove content that violates our guidelines.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. We don't collect personal information. The app uses anonymous accounts — no login required. See our [Privacy Policy](/holdit-legal/privacy-policy) for details.

**What languages are supported?**
HoldIt is available in English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, German, and Arabic.
